<h2>EZlabpure™ Hydrophobic PP Syringe Filters – 13mm, 0.22µm Non-Sterile for Fast Filtration of Water-Based &amp; Solvent Samples in Lab Workflow</h2>
<div id="description" class="tab-content active">
<p>EZlabpure™ syringe filters are ideal for processing both water-based and solvent-based solutions, providing superior chemical compatibility and rapid filtration efficiency.</p>
<p>Available in multiple membrane types with pore sizes of 0.2 µm or 0.45 µm, each filter is built with a durable polypropylene casing and features a female Luer-Lok™ inlet and male Luer slip outlet for secure, leak-proof connections.</p>
<p>All materials comply with USP Class VI Standards and are available for both sterile and non-sterile applications.</p>
